Company Dashboard

This side list is informational and describes the data each company can manage inside Fleet Protect 365.

Inspections

Drivers perform both pre-trip and post-trip inspections as pass or fail. If an inspection fails, the issue is fully documented and linehaul, the shop, and dispatch are notified.

Drivers

An active list of current drivers using the program.

Vehicles

A list of company trucks and unit numbers. In the driver app, drivers select the truck assigned for that day.

Trailers

Drivers enter the trailer number or trailer numbers they are taking on that day's run.

Dollies

For doubles, drivers enter the dolly number used between trailers.

Documents

User guides, employee handbook, legal documents, company policies, onboarding files, and other company files.

Reports

Drivers can view trip submissions for the current day, week, or month.

Alerts

Messages from management for weather alerts, road closures, safety updates, dispatch notices, and company announcements.

Users

Non-driver users with access, including compliance officers, safety, supervisors, dispatch, admins, and management.
